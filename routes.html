Route of Emotions

5.0 ★

  • • Duration: 10 Days
  • • Transport: 4x4 Jeep
  • • Vibe: Discovery
Day 1-2: São Luís Day 3-4: Lençóis Maranhenses Day 5-6: Parnaíba Delta Day 7-8: Barra Grande Day 9-10: Jericoacoara

Description: An off-road adventure crossing three states. Discover the white dunes of Lençóis Maranhenses, the Parnaíba Delta, and the paradise beaches of Jericoacoara.

Days 1-2: Explore colonial São Luís, staying in a heritage mansion in the historic center. Days 3-4: Traverse the iconic dunes of Lençóis Maranhenses, resting in a charming local pousada. Days 5-6: Navigate the Parnaíba Delta by boat to spot wildlife, sleeping in a riverside lodge. Days 7-8: Relax or kitesurf in Barra Grande, enjoying a beach bungalow. Days 9-10: End in paradise at Jericoacoara, watching the sunset from a village inn.

Colonial Heritage

4.8 ★

  • • Duration: 9 Days
  • • Stay: Boutique Inns
  • • Focus: Culture
Day 1-2: Salvador Day 3-4: Pelourinho Day 5-6: Recôncavo Day 7-8: Cachoeira Day 9: Praia do Forte

Description: A colorful journey through Brazil's soul. Explore the cobbled streets of Salvador de Bahia and historic gold towns, immersing yourself in Afro-Brazilian culture and music.

Days 1-2: Arrive in vibrant Salvador, staying in a boutique hotel near the bay. Days 3-4: Walk the historic Pelourinho district to experience live percussion. Days 5-6: Take a scenic drive to the Recôncavo region, the birthplace of samba. Days 7-8: Explore the colonial architecture of Cachoeira, resting in a riverside inn. Day 9: Conclude at Praia do Forte, relaxing by the sea and visiting the turtle sanctuary.
